# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Tanabata respectful, inclusive, and free from harassment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or expression.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and constructive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept feedback professionally.
- Prioritize the community and project users over personal disagreements.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, threats, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report violations through the repository's security advisory channel. Maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, limit participation, or permanently ban participants according to the severity and recurrence of conduct.

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html). Its Community Impact Guidelines inform enforcement decisions.
